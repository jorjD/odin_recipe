Lemon Blueberry Pound Cake

Ingredients

  • Blueberries
  • Lemons
  • Sugar
  • Egss
  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Confectionery Sugar
  • Salt
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Milk

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Line or grease your cake pan with parchment
  3. Mix all the dry ingredients in a bowl
  4. Mix all the wet ingredients using an electric mixer
  5. Slowly incorporate the dry mixture into the wet batter until just combined.
  6. Toss the blueberries in a tablespoon of flour in a bowl.
  7. Add the blueberries to the batter and getnly stir them in.
  8. Pour the batter into the cake pan and bake for 55-65 mins.
  9. Make the glaze by whisking the confectionery sugar with lemon juice and lemon zest
  10. Once the cake is cooked, let it cool outside its pan and pour the glaze on top when just warm.
