Banana Bread

Recipe from Entertaining with Beth

Ingredients

  • 4 very ripe Bananas
  • 3 eggs
  • 3/4 cup of Canola or Vegetable Oil
  • 1/4 cup of Water
  • 1/2 cup of Sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of Vanilla Exract
  • 2 1/2 cups of Flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of Salt
  • 2 teaspoons of baking soda
  • 1/2 Bittersweet Chocolate
  • 1/2 cup of Pecans/Walnuts

Directions

  • Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  • Grease your loaf pan
  • Mix all the dry ingredients in a bowl
  • Mash the bananas and mix in the wet ingredients well
  • Incorporate the dry mixture in 3 batches until just combined
  • Pour your batter into the loaf pan and bake for around 50 mins(use the toothpick test)
  • Let the bread cool once fully cooked and serve when slightly warm
