Pita

Recipe from LOVE & LEMONS

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup of warm water
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons of Yeast
  • 1 tablespoon plus 1 teaspoon of Sugar
  • 3 3/4 cups of Bread Flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of Salt
  • 3 tablespoons of Olive Oil
  • 3/4 cups of Whole Greek Yoghurt

Directions

  • Mix the water, yeast, and the teaspoon of sugar in a bowl and let it sit for around 5 mins to make sure the yeast is active
  • Mix the flour, salt and the tablespoon of sugar in a bowl
  • Add the oil and yoghurt to the yeast water and mix
  • Slowly incorporate the wet mixture into the dry
  • Knead for around 7-10 mins until soft and slightly sticky
  • Let the dough rise in a oiled bowl for at least 2 hours
  • Once the dough has risen enough, heat up a cast iron pan
  • Roll out the balls into a circular plane about 1/2 inch thick
  • Cook the bread on each side until slightly browned
